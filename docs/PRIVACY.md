# Phingo English — Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** May 27, 2026

Phingo English helps Kurdish speakers learn English. This document mirrors the in-app Privacy Policy (Settings → Privacy Policy).

## Summary

- **Free app** — no paid subscriptions in the current release.
- **On-device data** — progress, XP, streak, language, font, and settings are stored on your device.
- **Microphone** — used for speaking practice; not uploaded to our servers by default.
- **No user accounts** in this version.

## Data on your device

We store locally: lesson progress, XP, daily goal progress, streak, app language, Kurdish font choice, optional AI Teacher history, and preferences.

## Microphone & speech

Voice features use the device microphone and OS speech recognition. Audio is not sent to Phingo servers unless you configure an optional third-party AI API (developer feature).
